About Us

The R&D team is a sub-department that has been separated from the Project Marketing department. Their work mainly focuses on programming and learning about new innovative equipment. The team specializes in PLC, C#, and other technologies. As for the equipment, it includes IAI, Festo, KUKA, and others.
